My Most Favourite Movies

Here are the Top 3 Movies

The Avengers

This movie has a way of capturing the attention unlike any other. The superheroes are portrayed as beings with emotions and struggles and problems that any normal human being have. But unlike normal humans, they are also supposed to defend their planet while struggling with their personal life.

Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gham

This is a bollywood movie, starring Hrithik Roshan, Sharukh Khan, Amitab Bachan, Jaya Bachan, Kajol Rani Mukherjee and kareena Kapoor. There something about this movie that captures and touches the audience emotions. The way the actors a portrayed their charecters is nothing but magical.

Harry Potter

This is a movie from my childhood till adulthood. The movie is about the journey of a young troubled boy completing his magic school by overcoming hardships and threats.
